Introduction

Phish Protect is your ultimate shield against phishing attacks and malicious exploits. By analyzing your browser’s behavior in real-time, it helps detect phishing pages and blocks BEEF (Browser Exploitation Framework) attacks before they can compromise your security.

  • Availble On: Chrome Web Store
  • Tested On: Google Chrome, Chromium, Microsoft Edge
How It Works

Simply pin the Phish Protect extension and click the icon in your Chrome toolbar to analyze your current webpage. Phish Protect will provide detailed security insights, including:

  • ✅ Phishing Detection: Evaluates URLs and domains for suspicious activity.
  • ✅ Trusted Domain Check: Identifies if a website is in your trusted list.
  • ✅ SSL & TTL Status: Verifies secure connections and domain legitimacy.
  • ✅ Form Analysis: Detects hidden or suspicious login forms.
  • ✅ IP Behavior Monitoring: Flags unusual IP activity linked to phishing sites.
  • ✅ BEEF Exploit Protection: Detects and blocks browser exploitation attempts.
Additional Features
  • Manage Trusted Domains: Easily add/remove domains in the options page.
  • Background Security Checks: Enable/disable automatic alerts for untrusted sites.
  • Quick Trust List Access: Add sites to your trusted list directly from the popup.
